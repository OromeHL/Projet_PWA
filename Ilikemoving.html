Goal

Normal Mode :

Difficulty is defined at the beginning and does not change.
You earn points by killing enemies.

Survival Mode :

Asylum of the Daleks :

Difficulty is easy at first, but increases with time.
You earn points by killing enemies and by staying alive.

Into the Dalek :

Difficulty is easy at first, but increases with time.
You earn points by staying alive.
Beware : YOU CANNOT ATTACK !

Controls

How to play :

Use the directional keys in order to move.
Press Space in order to shoot.

Keyboard Shortcuts :

P : Run/pause the game
R : Start new game
S : Enable/disable sound
